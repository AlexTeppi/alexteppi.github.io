About Forest News

ስለ ፎረስት ኒውስ

Dedicated to providing accurate, timely, and impactful news about forest conservation, environmental issues, and sustainable practices in Ethiopia and beyond.

በኢትዮጵያ እና ከዚያ ባሻገር ስለ ደን ጥበቃ፣ የአካባቢ ጉዳዮች፣ እና ዘላቂ ልምዶች ትክክለኛ፣ ወቅታዊ፣ እና ተጽዕኖ ፈጣሪ ዜናዎችን ለመስጠት የተሰጠ።

Our Mission

ተልዕኮአችን

Forest News is dedicated to raising awareness about the critical importance of forest conservation and environmental protection in Ethiopia and globally. We believe that informed communities are empowered communities, and through our bilingual reporting, we aim to bridge knowledge gaps and inspire action across language barriers.

ፎረስት ኒውስ በኢትዮጵያ እና በዓለም አቀፍ ደረጃ ስለ ደን ጥበቃ እና የአካባቢ ጥበቃ ወሳኝ ጠቀሜታ ግንዛቤ ለማሳደግ የተሰጠ ነው። መረጃ ያላቸው ማህበረሰቦች ብቃት ያላቸው ማህበረሰቦች እንደሆኑ እናምናለን፣ እና በሁለት ቋንቋ ዘገባችን አማካኝነት፣ የእውቀት ክፍተቶችን ለማገናኘት እና በቋንቋ እንቅፋቶች ዙሪያ እርምጃ ለማነሳሳት እንሻለን።

Our reporting focuses on the intersection of environmental science, indigenous knowledge, policy, and community action. We strive to highlight solutions alongside challenges, celebrating conservation successes while honestly addressing the threats facing our natural world.

የእኛ ዘገባ በአካባቢ ሳይንስ፣ የአካባቢ እውቀት፣ ፖሊሲ፣ እና የማህበረሰብ እርምጃ መጣመሪያ ላይ ያተኩራል። ከፈተናዎች ጎን ለጎን መፍትሄዎችን ለማጉላት እንጥራለን፣ የጥበቃ ስኬቶችን በማክበር በተፈጥሮ ዓለማችን ፊት ያሉትን ስጋቶች በታማኝነት እናስተናግዳለን።

Our Team

Alemayehu Alemu

Founder & Editor-in-Chief መስራች እና ዋና አዘጋጅ

Alemayehu is an environmentalist and forestry expert working with the Ethiopian government in the Sheka zone, Teppi town. With extensive experience in forest conservation and community engagement, he founded Forest News to bridge the gap between scientific knowledge and public awareness about environmental issues in Ethiopia.
